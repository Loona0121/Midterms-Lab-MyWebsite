The Graham Effect

Elle Kennedy

Genre: Romance

Published: 2023

The novel follows Gigi Graham, daughter of hockey legend Garrett Graham, as she strives to qualify for the women's national hockey team and win Olympic gold. To enhance her performance behind the net, she seeks assistance from Luke Ryder, a formidable and aloof player who has recently become co-captain of Briar University's men's hockey team following a merger with a rival program. In exchange for his coaching, Gigi agrees to advocate for Ryder to secure a coveted summer coaching position with her father.

As Gigi and Ryder spend more time together, their professional arrangement evolves into a complex relationship filled with undeniable chemistry and personal challenges. Both must navigate their ambitions, family expectations, and the intense world of collegiate hockey, all while confronting their growing feelings for each other. The Graham Effect explores themes of self-discovery, legacy, and the delicate balance between personal and professional aspirations.
