Things We Leave Unfinished

Rebecca Yarros

Genre: Romance

Published: 2021

After a painful divorce, Georgia Stanton returns to her late grandmother’s home, inheriting the unfinished manuscript of the famous romance author Scarlett Stanton. When Noah Harrison, a bestselling novelist, is hired to complete the book, Georgia is reluctant to let him touch her grandmother’s work. However, as they clash over the manuscript’s ending, sparks fly between them, and Georgia begins to open her heart again.

Interwoven with Georgia and Noah’s story is Scarlett’s own tale of love during World War II, a romance that was tragically cut short. As the past and present collide, Georgia and Noah must decide whether to hold onto their fears or embrace a love story of their own. Things We Leave Unfinished is an emotional, dual-timeline romance about second chances, heartbreak, and the power of unfinished stories.
