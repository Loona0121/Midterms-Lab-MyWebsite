Six of Crows

Leigh Bardugo

Genre: Fantasy

Published: 2015

In the bustling city of Ketterdam, a hub of international trade and crime, criminal prodigy Kaz Brekker is offered a lucrative but seemingly impossible heist—break into the impenetrable Ice Court and rescue a scientist who holds the secret to a dangerous drug. To pull off the mission, Kaz assembles a crew of outcasts and misfits: Inej, a silent spy known as the Wraith; Jesper, a sharpshooter with a gambling addiction; Nina, a Grisha Heartrender with powerful magic; Matthias, a former soldier with a vendetta against Grisha; and Wylan, a runaway explosives expert with a privileged past. Each member has their own motivations, secrets, and reasons for taking the job, but success could mean a fortune beyond their wildest dreams.

As the team navigates treacherous waters—both literally and figuratively—they must overcome internal conflicts and their own dark pasts while executing the elaborate heist. The Ice Court, a fortress guarded by ruthless forces, proves to be even more dangerous than anticipated, and tensions rise as betrayals, unexpected alliances, and hidden agendas come to light. Meanwhile, personal stakes grow higher, with Inej fighting for her freedom, Nina and Matthias facing their complicated history, and Wylan grappling with his father's dark legacy.

With adrenaline-pumping action and intricate character dynamics, Six of Crows delivers a thrilling adventure filled with twists and high-stakes gambits. As the crew fights against impossible odds, their survival depends on trust, skill, and Kaz’s ability to outthink his enemies. However, even if they manage to pull off the heist, escaping with their lives—and their loyalty to each other—proves to be an even greater challenge.
