Golden Son

Pierce Brown

Genre: Fantasy

Published: 2015

After infiltrating the elite Gold society, Darrow has risen through the ranks, but the true battle for power is just beginning. As a favored protégé of the powerful ArchGovernor Nero au Augustus, he plays a dangerous game, navigating betrayals and deadly politics while secretly working to dismantle the oppressive system from within. However, the ruling class is more fractured and treacherous than he ever imagined, and one wrong move could mean his downfall.

When war erupts among the Golds, Darrow must choose his allies carefully, forging uneasy partnerships with friends and foes alike. As he struggles to stay ahead of ruthless enemies, his revolution grows beyond what he can control, forcing him to question his own identity and the price of victory. Golden Son is a thrilling sequel filled with shocking twists, betrayals, and large-scale battles that push Darrow closer to either ultimate triumph—or devastating ruin.
