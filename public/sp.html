The Silent Patient

Alex Micaheledis

Genre: Mystery

Published: 2019

Alicia Berenson is a famous painter living in London with her husband, Gabriel, a renowned fashion photographer. They seem to have the perfect marriage—until one evening, Alicia shoots Gabriel five times in the face and never speaks another word. Her shocking act of violence turns her into a mystery, capturing public fascination as she is placed in a secure psychiatric facility, refusing to explain why she did it.

Theo Faber, a criminal psychotherapist, is determined to unravel Alicia’s silence. Obsessed with uncovering the truth, he takes a job at The Grove, the facility where she is being held. As he digs into Alicia’s past, he becomes entangled in her life in ways he never expected. With each revelation, Theo begins to question everything—about Alicia, her crime, and even himself.

A gripping psychological thriller with shocking twists, The Silent Patient is a haunting exploration of trauma, obsession, and the dark secrets hidden within the human mind. Its unpredictable ending will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew.
