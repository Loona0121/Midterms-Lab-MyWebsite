Red Rabbit

Alex Grecian

Genre: Horror

Published: 2023

Red Rabbit by Alex Grecian was published in 2023 and is a supernatural horror Western set in post-Civil War Kansas. The story follows a diverse group of characters, including a grieving widow, two cowboys, and a witch hunter accompanied by his mute ward. They set out on a dangerous journey to track down Sadie Grace, a notorious witch with a bounty on her head. However, their pursuit leads them into a treacherous landscape haunted by ghosts, demons, and supernatural forces far beyond their understanding.

As the group ventures deeper into the unknown, they must confront not only the horrors lurking in the wilderness but also their own dark pasts. The lines between hunter and hunted blur as they realize that Sadie may not be the true evil they should fear. Red Rabbit blends elements of folk horror, Western grit, and psychological suspense, delivering a chilling tale of survival, fate, and the supernatural forces that shape the world.
