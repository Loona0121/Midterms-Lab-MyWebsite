The Troop

Agustina Bazterrica

Genre: Horror

Published: 2014

The Troop by Nick Cutter is a chilling horror novel that blends survival, body horror, and psychological terror.

As the infection spreads, the boys must fight for survival, not only against the parasite but against each other. Fear, paranoia, and desperation take hold, revealing the darkest sides of human nature. Isolated with no hope of rescue, the troop must face the ultimate question: who will make it out alive?

Every year, Scoutmaster Tim Riggs takes his troop of boys on a camping trip to a remote island off the Canadian coast. But their routine adventure turns into a nightmare when an emaciated, sickly stranger stumbles into their camp.
