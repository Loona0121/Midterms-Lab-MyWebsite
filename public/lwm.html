Local Woman Missing

Mary Kubica

Genre: Mystery

Published: 2021

In a quiet suburban neighborhood, Shelby Tebow disappears without a trace. Soon after, another woman, Meredith Dickey, and her six-year-old daughter, Delilah, vanish as well. As fear spreads through the community, the cases grow cold, and people begin to move on—until, eleven years later, Delilah suddenly reappears.

Now a teenager, Delilah’s return raises more questions than answers. Where has she been all this time? What happened to her mother? And is her story really the truth? As the search for answers intensifies, dark secrets begin to unravel, revealing shocking twists that no one saw coming.

A gripping and intricately woven thriller, Local Woman Missing explores the dark underbelly of suburban life, the weight of buried secrets, and the lengths people will go to protect the ones they love. With a fast-paced narrative and alternating timelines, this psychological suspense novel keeps readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.
