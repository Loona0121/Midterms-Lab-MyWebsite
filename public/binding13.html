Binding 13

Chloe Walsh

Genre: Young Adult

Published: 2018

Shannon Lynch has always lived in survival mode. Coming from a troubled home and burdened with painful secrets, she hopes for a fresh start when she transfers to a prestigious private school. But fitting in isn’t easy, and when she catches the attention of the school’s golden boy—rugby star Johnny Kavanagh—her world turns upside down.

Johnny has everything: talent, charm, and a future in professional rugby. But beneath his confident exterior, he struggles with pressures both on and off the field. Drawn to Shannon’s quiet strength, he finds himself wanting to protect her, even as he battles his own demons. As their connection deepens, they realize that love isn’t always easy—it’s messy, complicated, and sometimes painful.

Filled with raw emotions, intense chemistry, and heart-wrenching moments, Binding 13 is a gripping coming-of-age story about love, trauma, and finding hope in unexpected places.
