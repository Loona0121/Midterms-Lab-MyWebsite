Jade City

Fonda Lee

Genre: Fantasy

Published: 2017

In the city of Janloon, jade grants its wearers enhanced abilities, making it the most coveted and dangerous resource in the world. The Kaul family, leaders of the No Peak clan, control much of the jade trade, but their dominance is threatened by the rival Mountain clan, which seeks to take power by any means necessary. As tensions rise, the Kaul siblings—loyal but disciplined Lan, reckless warrior Hilo, and the ambitious yet reluctant Shae—must navigate shifting alliances, betrayals, and brutal street warfare to protect their family’s legacy.

As the conflict escalates, old traditions clash with modern influences, and the fight for jade becomes a fight for survival. Each member of the Kaul family is forced to make difficult choices that test their loyalty, strength, and beliefs. Jade City is a gripping mix of crime drama and fantasy, blending martial arts action, political intrigue, and the weight of family duty in a world where power is measured in jade.
