Harry Potter

J.K. Rowling

Genre: Fantasy

Published: 1997

The Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling follows the journey of Harry Potter, an ordinary boy who learns on his 11th birthday that he is actually a wizard. He is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he discovers a magical world filled with spells, potions, mythical creatures, and hidden dangers. Harry quickly befriends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and together, they navigate the challenges of school life, uncover magical secrets, and encounter dark forces that threaten the wizarding world.

As Harry learns more about his past, he discovers that he is famous for surviving an attack from the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, who murdered his parents but mysteriously failed to kill him as a baby. Over the years, Harry faces increasing dangers as Voldemort regains strength and seeks to reclaim power. Alongside his friends, his wise mentor Albus Dumbledore, and the members of the Order of the Phoenix, Harry fights to stop Voldemortâ€™s rise, leading to an ultimate battle between good and evil.

The series, spanning seven books, explores themes of friendship, bravery, love, sacrifice, and destiny, as Harry embraces his role as the chosen one while uncovering the mysteries of his past and the true power of magic.
