Onyx Storm

Rebecca Yarros

Genre: Fantasy

Published: 2021

Ren, a skilled con artist, arrives in the city of Nadežra with a daring plan—to infiltrate one of its noble houses and secure a future of wealth and security. Disguising herself as a long-lost relative, she inserts herself into House Traementis, a struggling aristocratic family, hoping to gain their trust and fortune. However, as she navigates the treacherous world of high society, she soon realizes that the city's tangled web of secrets, power struggles, and hidden magic is far more dangerous than she anticipated.

As the conflict escalates, old traditions clash with modern influences, and the fight for jade becomes a fight for survival. Each member of the Kaul family is forced to make difficult choices that test their loyalty, strength, and beliefs. Jade City is a gripping mix of crime drama and fantasy, blending martial arts action, political intrigue, and the weight of family duty in a world where power is measured in jade.As Ren plays her long con, she becomes entangled with powerful enemies, underground rebels, and a masked vigilante known as the Rook. The deeper she goes, the more she questions whether she’s the predator or the prey in this game of deception. The Mask of Mirrors is a lush, intrigue-filled fantasy that blends courtly politics, magical mysteries, and a compelling cast of characters in a city where illusion and reality are often one and the same.
