Better than the Movies

Lynn Painter

Genre: Young Adult

Published: 2021

Liz Buxbaum has always been a hopeless romantic, believing that life should play out like her favorite rom-coms. She’s had a crush on Michael, the charming boy next door, for years, and when he moves back to town, she sees it as the perfect opportunity to finally live out her love story. But there’s just one problem—Michael barely notices her.

Desperate to catch his attention, Liz strikes a deal with her annoying, cocky neighbor, Wes Bennett. Though Wes has always been her childhood nemesis, he seems to have an effortless way of getting along with everyone, including Michael. As Wes helps her get closer to her crush, Liz starts to realize that real-life love stories don’t always follow the perfect script—and sometimes, the best romances are the ones you never see coming.

Full of witty banter, heartfelt moments, and classic rom-com nostalgia, Better Than the Movies is a charming YA romance about love, friendship, and discovering that sometimes the perfect story is the one you didn’t expect.
