Book Lovers

Emily Henry

Genre: Romance

Published: 2022

Nora Stephens is a sharp, ambitious literary agent who thrives in the fast-paced world of publishing. When her sister, Libby, convinces her to take a month-long trip to the small town of Sunshine Falls, North Carolina, Nora reluctantly agrees, hoping for a change of pace. However, instead of finding a quaint romantic escape, she keeps running into Charlie Lastraâ€”a brooding, no-nonsense book editor from New York who has always been her professional rival.

As their paths continue to cross, their competitive banter turns into something deeper, forcing Nora to reevaluate her carefully structured life. Meanwhile, she struggles to balance her own dreams with her deep sense of responsibility to her sister. Book Lovers is a witty, heartfelt romance that flips classic small-town romance tropes, celebrating ambition, family bonds, and the unexpected ways love can rewrite our stories.
