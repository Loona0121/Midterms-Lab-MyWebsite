Brother

Ania Ahlborn

Genre:Horror

Published: 2015

Brother by Ania Ahlborn is a dark and disturbing horror novel that explores the twisted bonds of family, violence, and psychological torment.

Set in rural West Virginia, the novel follows 19-year-old Michael, who has grown up in an isolated, dysfunctional family with a horrifying secret—his older brother, Rebel, and the rest of the family abduct, torture, and murder young women for sport. Unlike the others, Michael is tormented by guilt and dreams of escaping this nightmare, especially after meeting a girl who shows him kindness and a glimpse of a different life.

But leaving isn’t easy, especially when Rebel always seems to be watching, and family ties run deep—sometimes, too deep to break. As Michael struggles with his own identity and morality, he’s forced to confront a chilling truth: no one escapes the Morrow family unscathed.
