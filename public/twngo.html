Things We Never Got Over

Lucy Score

Genre: Romance

Published: 2022

Naomi Witt arrives in the small town of Knockemout, Virginia, expecting to help her estranged twin sister, Tina, only to be blindsided when Tina steals her car and money—leaving behind an 11-year-old niece Naomi never knew existed. Stranded with no job, no home, and suddenly responsible for a child, Naomi is forced to rely on the gruff but irresistibly attractive Knox Morgan, a local bad-boy bar owner who prefers solitude over complications.

Despite their clashing personalities, Knox steps in to help Naomi navigate her new reality, and their reluctant partnership soon sparks an undeniable attraction. As Naomi works to rebuild her life, uncover the truth about her sister’s past, and care for her niece, she and Knox must confront their growing feelings. Things We Never Got Over is a heartwarming yet steamy romance filled with humor, small-town charm, and second chances at love.
