The Sword of Kaigen

M.L. Wang

Genre: Fantasy

Published: 2022

Set in a world of elemental warriors, The Sword of Kaigen follows Misaki and her son Mamoru, members of the Matsuda family, who are known for their mastery of ice-based combat. Mamoru, a dedicated warrior-in-training, dreams of proving his strength, while Misaki struggles with the life she left behind—one filled with freedom and a past she has kept hidden. When an unexpected invasion threatens their homeland, both mother and son are forced to confront the brutal realities of war, challenging everything they believed about honor, duty, and their own abilities.

As battle erupts, Misaki’s buried skills resurface, revealing a fierce warrior beneath her role as a wife and mother. Meanwhile, Mamoru faces the harsh truths of war, far beyond the ideals he was taught. Through heartbreak and sacrifice, The Sword of Kaigen delivers a gripping tale of family, resilience, and the strength it takes to defy fate in the face of overwhelming destruction.
