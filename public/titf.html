Tender Is the Flesh

Agustina Bazterrica

Genre: Horror

Published: 2020

Tender Is the Flesh by Agustina Bazterrica is a chilling dystopian novel that explores the darkest depths of human nature and society’s capacity for moral decay.

Set in a near-future world where animal meat has become contaminated and unsafe to eat, the government legalizes the breeding and consumption of human meat. Marcos Tejo, who works at a processing plant for this new industry, has become numb to the horrors around him—until he is given a live "specimen" as a gift. As he forms an unexpected connection with her, Marcos begins to question the world he has accepted and whether he can continue to participate in its brutality.
