Butcher and Blackbird

Brynne Weaver

Genre: Romance

Published: 2023

The novel follows Sloane Sutherland, known as the "Orb Weaver," and Rowan Kane, dubbed the "Boston Butcher," both of whom are serial killers targeting other serial killers. Their paths cross when Rowan discovers Sloane trapped with her latest victim. Recognizing their shared dark pursuits, they form an unusual bond and establish an annual competition to hunt and eliminate dangerous predators across the United States.

As their partnership evolves, Sloane and Rowan navigate a complex relationship that challenges their perceptions of love and morality. Their deepening connection forces them to confront their pasts and the ethical implications of their actions. Butcher & Blackbird blends elements of dark romance, suspense, and psychological exploration, offering readers a provocative narrative that delves into the complexities of justice and affection amidst a backdrop of vigilantism.
