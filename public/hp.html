Happy Place

Emily Henry

Genre: Romance

Published: 2023

Harriet and Wyn were the perfect couple—until they secretly broke up six months ago. The problem? They haven’t told their best friends yet. When their annual getaway to a cherished Maine cottage arrives, they decide to keep up the act, pretending to still be together so they don’t ruin the trip for their tight-knit friend group. Forced to share a room and relive old memories, the lines between pretending and reality begin to blur.

As the week unfolds, Harriet and Wyn confront the reasons behind their breakup, their unresolved feelings, and whether love can be rekindled after heartbreak. Happy Place is a deeply emotional and nostalgic story about love, friendship, and the difficulty of letting go—reminding us that sometimes, the right person has been there all along.
