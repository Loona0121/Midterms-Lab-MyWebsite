Wild Side

Elsie Silver

Genre: Romance

Published: 2025

The novel centers on Tabitha, who becomes the guardian of her three-year-old nephew, Milo, after her sister Erika's passing. To secure custody amid a legal challenge, Tabitha enters a marriage of convenience with Rhys Dupris, her late sister's enigmatic neighbor. Rhys is a secretive and brooding man whose work often takes him away for weeks, returning with mysterious bruises he refuses to explain. Their relationship is initially strained, filled with palpable tension and unspoken attraction.

As they navigate their unconventional arrangement, Tabitha begins to see a different side of Rhysâ€”a fierce, protective, and unexpectedly gentle man who becomes a steadfast presence for both her and Milo. The forced proximity challenges their initial perceptions, leading to a deepening bond that blurs the lines between pretense and genuine affection. Wild Side explores themes of love, trust, and the transformative power of family, delivering a heartfelt narrative that resonates with emotional depth.
