Then She Was Gone

Lisa Jewell

Genre: Mystery

Published: 2017

Ellie Mack was fifteen, smart, popular, and seemingly had a bright future ahead—until she vanished without a trace. Her disappearance devastated her family, leaving her mother, Laurel, searching for answers for years. A decade later, Laurel is still struggling to move on when she meets Floyd, a charming man who sweeps her off her feet. But when she meets his nine-year-old daughter, Poppy, Laurel is shocked—Poppy looks exactly like Ellie.

As Laurel grows closer to Floyd and his family, unsettling questions begin to surface. Who is Floyd, really? What happened to Ellie all those years ago? And why does Poppy bear such an uncanny resemblance to her missing daughter? As Laurel digs deeper, she unravels a chilling mystery that will change everything she thought she knew

A gripping psychological thriller filled with shocking twists and deep emotional layers, Then She Was Gone explores grief, obsession, and the lengths people will go to uncover the truth.
