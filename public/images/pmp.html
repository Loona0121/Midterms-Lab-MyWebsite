Practice Makes Perfect

Sarah Adams

Genre: Romance

Published: 2023

Practice Makes Perfect by Sarah Adams is a charming small-town romance filled with humor, heart, and swoon-worthy moments.

Annie Walker, a sweet and practical florist in Rome, Kentucky, is ready for love—but the right kind of love. She dreams of a steady, predictable romance, but her lack of dating experience makes finding "the one" difficult. Enter Will Griffin, a tattooed, brooding bodyguard who is in town for a temporary job. With his effortless charm and experience, he agrees to help Annie practice dating, showing her what chemistry and attraction really feel like.

But as their "practice" lessons turn into something more real, Annie and Will must face their growing feelings and decide if opposites really do attract—or if love was never meant to be easy.
