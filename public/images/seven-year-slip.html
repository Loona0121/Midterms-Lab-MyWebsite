The Seven Year Slip

Ashely Poston

Genre: Romance

Published: 2023

The Seven Year Slip by Ashley Poston is a heartwarming romantic novel with a touch of magical realism. It follows Clementine, a book-loving publicist who is struggling with grief after losing her beloved aunt. When she moves into her aunt’s old apartment, she discovers something extraordinary—the apartment occasionally slips seven years into the past.

One day, Clementine meets a charming, passionate young chef named Iwan in the apartment. The catch? He’s living seven years in the past. As their connection deepens, she must confront the impossible reality of their relationship and decide whether love can defy time itself.

Filled with emotion, fate-driven romance, and self-discovery, The Seven Year Slip is a beautifully written story about love, loss, and the magic of timing.
