Archer's Voice

Mia Sheridan

Genre: Romance

Published: 2014

Archer’s Voice by Mia Sheridan is a deeply emotional romance about healing, love, and finding one’s voice. The story follows Bree Prescott, a young woman seeking a fresh start in the quiet town of Pelion, Maine, after experiencing a traumatic past. There, she meets Archer Hale, a mysterious and reclusive man who has lived in silence for years due to a childhood tragedy that left him unable to speak.

Despite his isolation, Bree is drawn to Archer’s kindness and vulnerability, and as she slowly breaks through his walls, their connection deepens into an intense, life-changing love. But both Bree and Archer must confront their pasts if they want a future together.

A beautifully written, heart-wrenching romance, Archer’s Voice explores themes of trauma, communication, and the power of unconditional love.
