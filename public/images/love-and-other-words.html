Love and Other Words

Christina Lauren

Genre: Romance

Published: 2018

Love and Other Words by Christina Lauren is a heartfelt second-chance romance that explores love, loss, and the power of words.

Macy Sorensen has built a life of stability, focused on her career and a practical relationship, keeping her emotions carefully guarded. But when she unexpectedly runs into Elliot Petropoulos—the boy who was once her best friend, her first love, and the person who broke her heart—old feelings resurface.

Told through alternating timelines, the story follows their past as childhood friends who bonded over their love of books and words, slowly becoming something more, and their present, where unresolved pain and longing linger. As Macy confronts the truth about why they fell apart, she must decide if she’s willing to open her heart again to the one person who has always understood her.
