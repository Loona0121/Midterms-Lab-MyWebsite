Yours Truly

Abby Jimenez

Genre: Romance

Published: 2023

Yours Truly by Abby Jimenez is a heartfelt romantic comedy that explores love, mental health, and second chances.

Dr. Briana Ortiz is going through a rough time—her divorce is almost finalized, her brother desperately needs a kidney transplant, and the last thing she needs is a new coworker who seems cold and distant. But when she unexpectedly receives a letter from Dr. Jacob Maddox, the quiet and socially anxious new doctor, everything changes.

What starts as a simple exchange of letters turns into an unexpected friendship, and when Jacob offers to be a kidney donor for Briana’s brother, she agrees to help him by pretending to date him to ease his family's pressure. But as their fake relationship turns into something real, Briana and Jacob must navigate their fears, insecurities, and past wounds to find happiness together.
