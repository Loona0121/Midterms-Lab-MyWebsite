Fourth Wing

Rebecca Yarros

Genre: Fantasy

Published: 2023

Fourth Wing by Rebecca Yarros is a thrilling fantasy novel set in the brutal world of Basgiath War College, where students train to become elite dragon riders. The story follows Violet Sorrengail, a young woman who has spent her life preparing to join the Scribe Quadrant—until her ruthless mother, a high-ranking general, forces her to enter the deadly Rider Quadrant instead.

Survival is anything but guaranteed, as dragons only bond with the strongest, and many cadets die before they even graduate. With her frail body and lack of combat training, Violet must outwit her enemies, including Xaden Riorson, the powerful and enigmatic leader of the rebellion-linked cadets. As war looms and dark secrets about their world unravel, Violet must prove she has what it takes to survive—or die trying

Packed with high-stakes action, dragon battles, and a slow-burning romance, Fourth Wing is a gripping start to The Empyrean series.
