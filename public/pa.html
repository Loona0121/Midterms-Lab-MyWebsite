Play Along

Liz Tomforde

Genre: Romance

Published: 2017

The story follows Roshelle, who, after a tumultuous night discovering her boyfriend's infidelity, inadvertently witnesses a murder. This perilous encounter leads to her abduction, and she awakens aboard a shipping container vessel, held captive by a crew of dangerous men. Among them is Mac, a rugged and enigmatic figure who becomes both her captor and protector. To survive, Roshelle must navigate this treacherous environment, using her wits and resilience to endure the harrowing circumstances.

As days turn into weeks, a complex relationship develops between Roshelle and Mac, filled with tension, mistrust, and an undeniable attraction. The boundaries between enemy and ally blur, leading to unexpected alliances and deepening emotions. Throughout their ordeal, secrets are unveiled, challenging their perceptions and forcing them to confront their pasts. Play Along weaves themes of survival, trust, and unexpected romance, set against a backdrop of danger and intrigue.
