Red Rising

Pierce Brown

Genre: Fantasy

Published: 2014

In a dystopian future where society is divided into a rigid color-based hierarchy, Darrow, a lowly Red, toils beneath the surface of Mars, believing his labor will help make the planet livable for future generations. However, when he discovers that the ruling Golds have been living in luxury on the planetâ€™s surface for years, he is thrust into a brutal rebellion. With the help of a secret resistance, Darrow undergoes a painful transformation to infiltrate the elite Gold society and dismantle their power from within.

Disguised as a Gold, Darrow enters the Institute, a ruthless proving ground where the next generation of rulers are trained through brutal warfare and psychological manipulation. As he battles cunning adversaries and forges uneasy alliances, he must balance his mission of vengeance with the need to survive. Red Rising is a gripping tale of rebellion, sacrifice, and the fight to break a system built on oppression, setting the stage for an epic revolution.
