The Will of the Many

James Islington

Genre: Fantasy

Published: 2023

The Will of the Many by James Islington is a gripping fantasy novel set in a world ruled by the Hierarchy, an empire that thrives on a system where the powerful siphon strength from those beneath them. The story follows Vis Telimus, a young orphan who hides his true identity while being forced into the elite Catenan Academy, where the next generation of rulers is trained.

To survive among the ruthless students, Vis must play their deadly games while secretly working against the system that seeks to control him. As he uncovers hidden plots and dangerous truths, he is drawn into a larger struggle for power, where loyalty is fragile, and deception is key.

Blending political intrigue, intense action, and a complex magic system, The Will of the Many is the first book in The Hierarchy series, perfect for fans of epic fantasy with deep world-building and high-stakes conflict.
