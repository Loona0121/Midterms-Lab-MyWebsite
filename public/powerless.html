Powerless

Lauren Roberts

Genre: Young Adult

Published: 2023

In the kingdom of Ilya, power defines status. The powerful—those gifted with supernatural abilities—rule, while the powerless are considered weak and disposable. Paedyn Gray has spent her entire life pretending to have abilities to survive in a society that would kill her if they knew the truth. As a thief living in the slums, she’s careful to stay under the radar—until a fateful encounter with the kingdom’s prince changes everything.

When Paedyn is mistakenly identified as an Elite, one of the powerful, she is thrown into the Purging Trials—an annual competition where the strongest fight for status and survival. Trapped among deadly competitors, including Prince Kai himself, Paedyn must play the game and deceive the entire kingdom or risk certain death. But as sparks fly between her and Kai, she struggles to keep up the lie while navigating the brutal world of the powerful.

With danger lurking at every turn and secrets that could bring down the kingdom, Paedyn must decide how far she is willing to go to protect herself—and whether love can truly exist in a world built on power and deception.
