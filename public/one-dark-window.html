One Dark Window

Rachel Gillig

Genre: Fantasy

Published: 2023

Elspeth Spindle has spent her life hiding a dangerous secret—she is infected with the Nightmare, a monstrous, sentient entity that lives within her mind. In the mist-laden kingdom of Blunder, magic is wielded through the powerful Providence Cards, but using them comes at a cost. Those who misuse magic risk infection, turning into something unnatural. Elspeth contracted the infection as a child and has kept it hidden ever since, relying on the Nightmare’s guidance and strength while fearing the moment it may take full control.

When Elspeth crosses paths with Ravyn Yew, the king’s nephew and a rogue highwayman, she is thrust into a desperate rebellion. Ravyn and his allies seek to collect all twelve Providence Cards in order to break the curse that has doomed Blunder, but doing so means defying the tyrannical king and facing ancient, forbidden magic. As Elspeth becomes more involved in their mission, she struggles to keep the Nightmare at bay while uncovering shocking truths about her past, her kingdom, and her own role in the prophecy that could change everything.

A gothic fantasy filled with dark romance, eerie magic, and haunting secrets, One Dark Window is the first book in The Shepherd King duology, blending high-stakes adventure with an unsettling, atmospheric tale of power and fate.
