The Only One Left

Riley Sager

Genre: Mystery

Published: 2023

In 1929, seventeen-year-old Lenora Hope was accused of brutally murdering her family at their isolated cliffside mansion in Maine. Though she never confessed, a chilling nursery rhyme about the killings spread through town, cementing her as a legend of horror. Decades later, Lenora remains at Hope’s End—now a paralyzed old woman, unable to speak—until she finally decides to share her story.

Kit McDeere, a struggling caregiver, is assigned to look after Lenora after a scandal leaves her desperate for work. When Lenora, using a typewriter, begins to reveal shocking details about what really happened the night of the murders, Kit finds herself caught in a web of secrets and lies. As eerie occurrences begin to unfold within the mansion’s decaying walls, Kit starts to wonder if Lenora is telling the truth—or if something far more sinister is at play.

A spine-chilling gothic thriller filled with dark family secrets, eerie atmospheres, and shocking twists, The Only One Left is a gripping tale of deception and survival that will keep readers guessing until the final page.
